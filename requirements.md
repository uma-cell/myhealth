# MyHealth - Requirements Document

## Project Overview

MyHealth is a smart healthcare application designed to help common users manage their health effectively. The application uses Artificial Intelligence to provide voice-based and text-based health assistance, making healthcare information accessible to everyone, including non-educated users.

## Problem Statement

Many people face difficulties in managing their daily health routines and accessing healthcare information. Common challenges include:

- Forgetting to take medicines on time
- Lack of awareness about healthy lifestyle practices
- Difficulty in finding nearby hospitals during emergencies
- Limited access to health information in simple language
- Confusion about health insurance policies
- Need for immediate medical assistance during emergencies

## Solution Description

MyHealth provides a comprehensive solution by offering:

- AI-powered voice and text-based health assistance
- Automated medicine and diet reminders with notifications
- Voice search with intelligent reply suggestions
- Hospital locator for both local and international facilities
- Daily health tips and educational content
- Emergency ambulance calling feature
- Health insurance guidance
- Visual support with medicine images and health videos

## Functional Requirements

### 1. Voice and Text Assistant
- Users can ask health-related questions using voice or text
- AI provides intelligent responses with suggestions
- Support for image-based queries and responses

### 2. Medicine Management
- Set medicine reminders with custom schedules
- Notification alerts with ringtone
- Display medicine images with names
- Track medicine intake history

### 3. Diet Management
- Create diet plans and reminders
- Notification alerts for meal times
- Dietary suggestions based on health conditions

### 4. Health Information
- Daily health tips including yoga and exercises
- Precautionary measures for common health issues
- Healthy lifestyle videos
- Simple language explanations

### 5. Hospital Finder
- Search nearby hospitals using location services
- Display hospital details and contact information
- Suggest foreign hospitals for advanced treatments
- Show directions to selected hospitals

### 6. Emergency Services
- Quick ambulance call button
- Emergency contact management
- Location sharing during emergencies

### 7. Health Insurance
- Guidance on health insurance policies
- Information about coverage and claims
- Simple explanations of insurance terms

## Non-Functional Requirements

### 1. Usability
- Simple and intuitive user interface
- Easy navigation for non-educated users
- Voice support for accessibility
- Multi-language support

### 2. Performance
- Fast response time for voice queries
- Quick loading of medicine and hospital information
- Reliable notification delivery

### 3. Security
- Secure storage of personal health data
- Privacy protection for user information
- Encrypted data transmission

### 4. Reliability
- Accurate medicine reminders
- Dependable emergency services
- Consistent AI responses

### 5. Compatibility
- Works on Android and iOS devices
- Supports various screen sizes
- Compatible with different network conditions

## Target Users

- Common people with basic or no education
- Elderly users who need health management support
- Patients with chronic conditions requiring regular medication
- Families managing health for multiple members
- Anyone seeking easy access to health information

## Conclusion

MyHealth addresses the critical need for accessible healthcare management by combining AI technology with user-friendly features. The application empowers users to take control of their health through timely reminders, easy access to information, and emergency support services. By focusing on simplicity and voice-based interaction, MyHealth ensures that healthcare assistance is available to everyone, regardless of their educational background.
